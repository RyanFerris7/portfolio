Ryan Ferris

Artist

BA Honours in Creative Digital Media

I am a college graduate with a passion for illustration, design and video game development.

As a self-taught illustrator I have studied anatomy / figure drawing and comic book art.

I draw inspiration from artists such as Yoji Shinkawa, HR Geiger and Masahiro Ito.

I have worked in a professional environment as a graphic designer. My tasks included:

  • Developing promotional content for the group's social media accounts.
  • Designing educational content for the group's online resources.
  • Creating motion graphic videos for the group.
  • Illustrating and designing posters to promote the group's own events.

I am proficient in software such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Clip Studio Paint.
